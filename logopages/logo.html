Logo Designs


The objective of my logo was to create a design that was simplistic, modern, and friendly. I decided to go with a design that uses my initials and chose to use a sans-serif typeface which gives the design a minimal, modern feel. The added heart and periwinkle color were chosen to create an approachable aura.
