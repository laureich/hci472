Typography

Merriweather & Lato

Major Third: type-scale.com
Base size: 1rem/16.00px

This design system uses Merriweather for the headings and Lato for the body text. Merriweather is an open source, serif typeface that was designed to be a text face that is easy to read on screens. Lato is an open source, sans-serif typeface that comes in 18 different styles and weights and is highly legible for a wide group of users, meeting accessibility requirements.
