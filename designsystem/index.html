About this Design System

Dependable, Genuine, Caring

This website provides an overview of my design system and presents the foundations, major components, and patterns used to style my portfolio website. I am dependable, genuine, and caring and my goal was to create a design system that depicts these qualities. I also appreciate a more simple, minimalistic look and wanted to incorporate that into my design system as well.
